Kory Becker - Business and Software Development Bio

Kory Becker is a Microsoft Certified and agile certified software developer and architect, providing C# ASP .NET web application implementation, database design, and mobile software development across a variety of business domains. Specializing in a range of .NET development technologies for clients in both the business and consumer sectors, with experience in the educational, healthcare, biopharmaceutical, financial, and utility industries. Kory Becker has developed a variety of award-winning software products, featured in prominent publications including PC Magazine (October 2002 Editors Choice Award), PC World (May 2006), USA Today (September 2003), and the Kim Komando Radio Show (August 2006). Software development articles authored by Kory Becker may be found syndicated online.
